Pigeonhole Principle Demonstration

Let n be the number of guests. Label the guests G₀, G₁, ..., Gₙ₋₁ and let guest Gᵢ order dish Dᵢ.

💡 The Pigeonhole Principle states that if we place n items into n-1 containers, at least one container must receive at least 2 items.
